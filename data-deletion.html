Delete Your Account & Data

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Coinzap account and the data associated with it, as required by Google Play's Data Safety policy.

How to request deletion

Coinzap does not yet have an in-app "Delete account" button. To request deletion, email us from the same email address you used to sign in with Google:

kanunga.lavesh@gmail.com

Include the words "Delete my Coinzap account" in your message. We will delete your account record and all associated data within 30 days and confirm by email once it's done.

What gets deleted

DataDeleted on request?
Google account link (Firebase Authentication record)Yes — permanently deleted
Synced game progress (coins, keys, streak, mission history) stored in our databaseYes — permanently deleted
Voucher claim history (used only to prevent duplicate/fraudulent claims)Yes — permanently deleted
Game progress stored locally on your deviceRemoved immediately by uninstalling the app, or clearing the app's storage in your device's Settings — no request needed
Advertising ID / ad interaction data collected by Google AdMobNot held by us — managed directly by Google. See Google's ad privacy controls

Partial deletion (keep playing as a guest)

If you'd rather just disconnect your Google account without deleting your progress, sign out from the Profile tab in the app — your progress stays saved on your current device (guest mode) but stops syncing to your Google account until you sign in again.

Questions
