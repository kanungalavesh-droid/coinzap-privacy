Privacy Policy for Coinzap

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Coinzap ("the App", "we", "us") is a rewards and entertainment app that lets users earn virtual coins and keys by watching advertisements and completing in-app activities, and redeem them for gift cards and vouchers. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.

  • Advertising identifiers — the App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob collects your device's advertising ID and related technical data (device type, general location derived from IP, app usage in connection with ads) to serve and measure ads. See Google's policy below.
  • Device identifier (app-generated) — the App generates a random identifier stored on your device to prevent abuse of reward limits (e.g. daily reward caps) and, if you redeem a real voucher, to process that redemption. This identifier is not tied to your name or contact details.
  • App activity — coins/keys earned, missions completed, and redemption history are stored locally on your device and, for real voucher redemptions, on our secure backend (Google Firebase) solely to fulfill the redemption and prevent duplicate/fraudulent claims.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate core features: tracking coins, keys, spins, missions, and streaks.
  • To display and personalize ads through Google AdMob.
  • To dispense real voucher/gift-card codes when you redeem a reward, and to prevent fraudulent or duplicate claims.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app reliability.

4. Data Retention

App activity data tied to your device identifier is retained only as long as needed to operate reward limits and honor redemptions, and is periodically purged. You can reset your local data at any time by clearing the App's storage/cache from your device settings or uninstalling the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Coinzap is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us using the details below and we will remove it.

6. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of ad personalization via your device's OS-level ad settings.
  • You can request deletion of any data associated with your device identifier by contacting us with that identifier (visible in the App's Settings screen, if applicable) or by uninstalling the App, which stops all further data collection from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
